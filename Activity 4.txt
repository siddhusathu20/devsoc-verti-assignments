User Journey Map:
1. Open the app
2. Search for food items
3. Scroll through available restaurants and choose one to buy from (Frustration! Potentially too many options to choose from - having a top recommendation helps.)
4. Select the item on the restaurant page and add it to your cart
5. View your cart and proceed to checkout
6. Pay using your selected payment method (Happiness! Order placed successfully.)
7. Wait for/track your order (Frustration! The order could take longer to deliver than expected.)
8. Collect order when delivered (Happiness! The order is delivered and the user gets to eat.)
